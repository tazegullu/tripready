Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how the TripReady mobile application handles user data.

Last updated: June 19, 2026

TripReady is a packing and travel checklist app designed to help users create trip checklists, packing lists, travel document reminders and before-you-go task lists.

TripReady does not require an account, does not use cloud sync and does not send trip or checklist data to an external server.

1. Personal Data Collected

TripReady does not ask users to provide their name, surname, email address, phone number, location, identity information, account information or similar personal data.

The app does not include user registration, login, membership or account creation.

2. Trip and Checklist Information Entered in the App

Users may enter trip-related information inside the app, such as:

  • Trip name
  • Destination
  • Travel date
  • Trip duration
  • Number of travelers
  • Selected packing list type
  • Custom packing list names
  • Custom checklist items
  • Checklist progress

This information is used only to create and manage packing and travel checklists on the user’s device.

This information:

  • Is stored locally on the user’s device
  • Is not sent to an external server
  • Is not shared with third parties
  • Is not sold, rented or used for advertising
  • Is not used to identify or track the user

3. Local Storage

TripReady stores trips, packing lists, custom checklist items, checklist progress and language preferences locally on the user’s device.

Because the app does not use cloud sync, this data is not automatically backed up or synchronized between devices by TripReady.

If the user deletes the app or resets the app data, locally stored TripReady data may be deleted.

4. Data Sharing

TripReady does not share, sell, rent or transfer user data to third parties.

The app does not use user data for behavioral advertising, profiling, tracking or analytics.

5. Advertising and Analytics

This version of TripReady does not include:

  • Advertising SDKs
  • Analytics SDKs
  • User tracking tools
  • Behavioral profiling
  • Remote data collection systems
  • Cloud backend services

If advertising, analytics, crash reporting, cloud sync or similar third-party services are added in a future version, this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly.

6. Permissions

TripReady does not request access to sensitive device permissions such as:

  • Location
  • Camera
  • Microphone
  • Contacts
  • Calendar
  • SMS
  • Photos
  • Files
  • Bluetooth
  • Health data

The app is designed to work as an offline checklist utility without requiring sensitive device access.

7. Children’s Privacy

TripReady is a general travel checklist utility.

The app does not knowingly collect personal data from children. Since TripReady does not require an account and does not collect personal data, children are not asked to submit personal information through the app.

If a parent or guardian believes that a child has contacted the developer and provided personal information by email, they may contact the developer to request deletion of that information.

8. Support and Contact by Email

If a user contacts the developer by email for support, feedback or questions, the user’s email address and message content may be used only to respond to that request.

Such email communication is not used for marketing, advertising or profiling.

Support emails may be retained as needed to respond to the user or comply with legal obligations.

9. Data Deletion

TripReady does not create user accounts and does not store user data on an external server.

Users can delete locally stored app data by:

  • Using the reset option inside the app, where available
  • Deleting trips or custom lists inside the app
  • Removing the app from their device

If a user has contacted the developer by email and wants that email communication deleted, they may contact the developer using the email address below.

10. Security

TripReady is designed to keep trip and checklist data on the user’s device.

However, no digital storage method can be guaranteed to be completely secure. Users are responsible for keeping their own devices secure.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time.

If changes are made, the updated Privacy Policy will be published on this page and the “Last updated” date will be revised.
